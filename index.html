How to make a sandwich

  1. Get bread.
  2. Get lunch meat.
  3. Get cheese.
  4. Get spicy brown mustard.
  5. Get a plate and knife.
  6. Place the meat on one slice of bread.
  7. Place the cheese on top of the meat.
  8. Use the knife to spread mustard on the other slice of bread.
  9. Put the slice of bread with mustard on top of the cheese/meat slice of bread.
  10. Put the sandwich on a plate.
